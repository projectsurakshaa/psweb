Our Vision

This portal aims at providing some aid to victims of domestic violence. In the times where the entire nation is facing lockdown for such extended periods, Domestic violence has seen an increasing number of cases majorly due to the frustration of people being cooped up in their houses. They are using other members of their family as an outlet for their anger and frustration which is highly wrong. with this project we hope to connect to those people and make them realise that they are not victims but victors.Our goal is to bridge the gap between the inflicted individuals and proper help.Through this project we aim at connecting you to all types of help that are possible to get you out of this situation.
